Services

We are working together to combat femicide and gender-based violence (GBV).

Community Awareness Campaigns

We organize educational campaigns to raise awareness about the impact of femicide and GBV, empowering communities to take action.

Online Support Network

We provide an online platform where survivors of GBV can connect, share experiences, and access support services.

Workshops and Training

Our workshops educate individuals and groups on identifying and preventing GBV, promoting safe and respectful relationships.

Legal Assistance

We offer resources and referrals to legal aid for survivors seeking justice and protection against GBV.

Helpline Support

Our dedicated helpline offers confidential support and guidance to those affected by femicide and GBV.

Advocacy and Policy Change

We advocate for stronger laws and policies to protect individuals from GBV and ensure accountability for perpetrators.
